AIFRED

A real-time mix diagnostic tool for producers who want useful feedback inside the session.

AIFRED is not a one-click mix generator or preset shortcut. It measures the audio that is actually playing, shows where the mix differs from the target, and keeps the decision in your hands.

The Halo system gives you a concise fingerprint for tone, width, loudness, punch, and reference behavior, with readable meters that stay grounded in real DSP instead of tiny jittery value changes.

Inside AIFRED, you can check:

  • tone balance
  • stereo width and mono behavior
  • loudness and peak behavior
  • punch and density
  • reference alignment
  • A/B comparison between your mix and a target

Instead of bouncing between random meters and trying to piece the story together in your head, AIFRED pulls it into one view so you can catch the problem faster and move with intention.

The goal is simple: less guessing, better decisions, cleaner mixes.

Why AIFRED Exists

Why AIFRED Exists

A lot of tools give you numbers. That doesn't always mean they give you clarity.

AIFRED was built because most producers do not need more clutter. They need a better read on what the mix is doing.

When you're deep in a session, the hardest part usually is not finding a plugin to throw on the chain. It's figuring out what actually needs attention first.

AIFRED helps you answer questions like:

  • Is this mix too narrow?
  • Is the top end pushing too hard?
  • Is it loud enough or just crushed?
  • Is it actually punching or just dense?
  • How far off is it from the reference?
  • What needs to be fixed first?

That is the point of the Halo. Not to look cool for no reason. To help you read the mix faster.

Free Online Mix Analyzer

Submit a reference candidate and get a quick read before you ever open the full plugin.

The online analyzer is a simple first-pass tool for checking where your mix is landing. Accepted submissions help build AIFRED's curated reference profile pool.

It helps you get a quick outside read on:

  • loudness
  • tonal balance
  • stereo behavior
  • general mix translation

It is not meant to replace the plugin. It is there to give you a quick direction check and help you catch obvious issues earlier.

Uploads receive a free limited analysis. Accepted references store analysis metadata through the reference intake path; this does not mean the beta plugin syncs cloud reference profiles automatically.
